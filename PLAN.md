One improvement I'd recommend

Instead of stopping at a database with queries, we'll design this like a real decision-support system. The project's centerpiece will be a Global Crisis Risk Index (GCRI) that combines indicators from the different datasets into a transparent, SQL-calculated score. This gives the project a clear business outcome and makes it much more compelling in interviews because you can explain not just how you built the database, but what decision it helps organizations make.
